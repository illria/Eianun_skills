# PS/Canva-made Thumbnail Style Guide

Use this guide whenever the generated image feels too AI-like, cinematic, over-rendered, or too polished in a synthetic way.

## Target feeling

The thumbnail should look like a Chinese creator made it manually in Photoshop, Canva, Figma, or 稿定设计:

- huge bold Chinese text first
- simple colored rectangles, rounded pills, and white capsules
- brand logo/product screenshot/card pasted into the layout
- clean hard shadows or 2D drop shadows, not cinematic light
- limited gradients, mostly flat blocks
- clear mobile readability over realism
- deliberate composition that feels assembled by a human designer

## Avoid AI-poster taste

Avoid these by default:

- cinematic lighting, lens flares, glowing world maps, excessive particle effects
- overly realistic 3D coins, glossy render objects, dramatic depth of field
- complex glassmorphism dashboards with too many tiny UI details
- hyper-polished product ad look
- random invented app screens with small unreadable text
- dramatic perspective, flying cards, holograms, smoke, volumetric light
- too much texture and too many visual elements

## Prefer these PS-like elements

- one flat or lightly-gradient background color field
- one obvious product/logo area on the right or top
- 2-3 text groups max
- thick white/black text outlines
- black or dark navy text shadows
- rounded white capsule for secondary message
- bright accent rectangle for the key phrase
- simple icons: bank, card, cloud, phone, shield, arrow
- screenshots or product cards shown front-on or only slightly tilted

## Prompt wording that works

Use phrases like:

- `looks like a manually designed Chinese YouTube thumbnail made in Photoshop/Canva`
- `flat graphic design, bold 2D typography, simple pasted product elements`
- `clean color blocks, rounded rectangles, thick outlined Chinese text`
- `not cinematic, not photorealistic, not glossy 3D, not AI poster art`
- `use realistic but simple product/logo mockups, like pasted assets in a creator thumbnail`

## Layout recipe

Default recipe:

1. Background: flat deep navy / green / blue / orange field with subtle geometric shapes.
2. Top badge: brand + short label, simple rounded rectangle.
3. Main text: 1-2 huge lines occupying half the canvas.
4. Support chips: one or two rounded capsules with keyword highlights.
5. Product area: logo, card, phone UI, app screenshot, or simple icon pasted on one side.
6. Effects: thick outline, flat shadow, very subtle glow only.

## Strong negative prompt block

Add this when outputs look too AI:

`Avoid cinematic poster style, photorealistic 3D render, glossy coins, lens flare, hologram, smoky lighting, dramatic depth of field, over-detailed fintech dashboard, tiny UI text, complex background, random invented product screen, excessive glow, and AI-generated ad look. Make it feel manually assembled in Photoshop/Canva with clean 2D shapes and bold text.`
