## Style guide excerpt
# Thumbnail Style Guide

This guide captures the style direction from the supplied successful examples.

## Visual DNA

- Chinese tutorial / tech / finance utility thumbnail.
- Large blocky Chinese typography is the main hero.
- Text must be readable when the thumbnail is tiny on mobile.
- 2-3 major text groups, not a paragraph.
- Brand/product cue on one side: app card, logo, phone, bank card, dashboard, map, device, cloud icon, or crypto exchange visual.
- Background uses bold color fields, gradients, glass cards, glow, depth, or subtle geometric shapes.
- High contrast with thick shadows or outline around text.
- Rounded pill labels are common for qualifiers like version, beginner, free, zero threshold, new policy, account opening, tutorial.

## Composition patterns

1. Left text / right product hero
   - left: huge hook text
   - right: product card, phone, app icon, logo, or banking card

2. Top badge / center huge title / bottom short proof
   - badge: version, zero threshold, latest policy
   - title: 4-10 large Chinese characters
   - proof: one concise supporting phrase

3. Split color panels
   - one product color dominates
   - secondary accent highlights keywords

4. Minimal high-impact text
   - one very large phrase, one supporting label, one product visual

## Text hierarchy

- Main hook: largest, 35-55% of canvas height across 1-2 lines.
- Secondary phrase: medium, often in a rounded capsule.
- Badge: small but bold, 1-5 words.

Good thumbnail text is not the full video title. It should be shorter and punchier.

## Color direction

Choose colors from the topic:

- banking/finance: teal, green, deep blue, white, dark navy
- crypto: yellow, black, wheat/gold, sky blue
- cloud/network: orange, blue, dark navy, cyan
- phone/eSIM: sky blue, white, magenta accent
- warning/policy: black, red, white

## Hard constraints

- No bottom red progress bar.
- Avoid tiny body text.
- Avoid more than three focal ideas.
- Avoid flat template-only output.
- Avoid exact copying of any reference thumbnail.


## Final output requirement
Generate a complete thumbnail image. Keep it sharp, high-contrast, mobile-readable, and visually richer than a simple SVG/Pillow mockup.
